## How to find interviewees quickly

- Post in Indie Hackers community ("I'm building a free AI spend audit tool — 10 min call to get feedback?")
- DM 5-10 founders on X who have tweeted about AI tool costs in the last 30 days
- Post in relevant Discord servers (Anthropic community, Cursor Discord, AI tools channels)
- LinkedIn message former classmates who work at early-stage startups
- Ask in YC Hacker News monthly "Who wants to be interviewed?" threads
- Post in university startup Slack/Discord communities

**Time estimate:** 2 hours total (30 min to find people, 45 min for 3 interviews, 45 min to write up notes)
